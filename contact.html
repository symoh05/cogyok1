Frequently Asked Questions

What time are your services?

Our main Shabbat service is every Saturday from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We also have midweek Bible study on Tuesdays at 6:00 PM.

Is there a dress code for services?

We encourage modest dress that honors Elohim. Many of our members choose to wear head coverings during worship, but this is not required.

Do I need to be Jewish to attend?

No! We welcome everyone who is interested in learning about Messiah from a Hebraic perspective. Our community includes people from all backgrounds.

How can I get involved in serving?

We have many opportunities to serve, from worship team to children's ministry to outreach. Contact our office or speak with one of our leaders after service to learn more.

Do you have programs for youth?

Yes! COGYOK is specifically our youth ministry for ages 13-30. We have weekly gatherings, monthly events, and special retreats throughout the year.
