Our Story

How COGYOK Came to Be

Our Humble Beginnings

COGYOK (COGMERS Youth of Kiambu) was founded in 2015 by a small group of young believers who were passionate about creating a space where Messianic youth could grow in their faith while embracing their Hebraic roots. What started as a weekly Bible study with just 5 members has now grown into a vibrant community of over 200 young people.

Our journey has been marked by Yahweh's faithfulness through every season. From meeting in living rooms to now having our own dedicated youth center, we've witnessed miraculous provision and growth that can only be attributed to His hand upon this ministry.

At the heart of COGYOK is a commitment to the truth of Scripture, the person of Yahshua Messiah, and the restoration of all things. We believe in raising up a generation that will walk in the fullness of their identity as set-apart believers.

2015

The First Gathering

Five young people began meeting weekly for Torah study and worship in a small apartment in Kiambu town.

2017

Official Recognition

COGMERS officially recognized COGYOK as its youth ministry arm, providing spiritual covering and support.

2019

First Youth Camp

We held our first annual youth camp with 50 attendees, marking a turning point in our growth and impact.

2022

Youth Center Acquired

Through generous donations, we purchased and renovated our current youth center facility.

Our Core Values

Torah-Centered

We believe the Torah is the foundation for righteous living and the revelation of Yahshua as Messiah. Our teachings and practices are rooted in Scripture from Genesis to Revelation.

Messianic Faith

Yahshua (Jesus) is the central focus of our faith - the promised Messiah who fulfilled the Torah, the Prophets, and the Writings. We proclaim His death and resurrection as the only way to salvation.

Authentic Community

We prioritize genuine relationships where members can be transparent, accountable, and supported in their spiritual journey. Our community is a family that loves unconditionally.

Cultural Relevance

While holding to biblical truth, we engage with our contemporary African context, demonstrating how ancient Hebraic faith applies to modern life in Kenya.

Servant Leadership

We follow Yahshua's example of servant leadership, where authority is exercised through humility, sacrifice, and care for those being led.

Spiritual Growth

We are committed to creating environments where every member can grow in their relationship with Elohim, develop their gifts, and fulfill their divine purpose.
