Our Story

COGYOK (COGMERS Youth of the Kingdom) was founded in 2010 as the youth ministry of Commonwealth of Greater Messianic Evangelical Restoration Synagogue (COGMERS). Our mission is to raise up a generation of young believers who love Yahshua Messiah and walk in obedience to the Torah.

We believe in the restoration of all things and that the covenants, commandments, and promises of Scripture are for all believers today. Our youth gather weekly to study Scripture, worship in Spirit and truth, and encourage one another in the faith.

Our name COGYOK reflects our identity as youth (YOK) belonging to COGMERS, called to be set apart for the Kingdom. We emphasize both our Messianic faith and our commitment to the Great Commission.
